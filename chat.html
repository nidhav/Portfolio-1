ExamGuardAI

The educational website developed by Nidhi Yadav, Mohit Ranjan, Nishant, and Tisha Dubey is designed to assist students in preparing for exams. The website features a user-friendly interface that leverages HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. One of the key highlights of the website is the integration of an AI chatbot, which provides personalized assistance to students.

Features

1. User-Friendly Interface:

The website has a clean and intuitive layout, making it easy for students to navigate through the resources.

2. AI Chatbot:

The integrated AI chatbot helps students with their queries, offers study tips, and provides instant feedback on practice questions.

3. Study Resources:

A wide range of study materials, including notes, practice questions, and video tutorials.

4. Responsive Design:

The website is fully responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across various devices.
